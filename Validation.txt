AI MISTAKE: The AI tried to look for the word "DownloadString" in the raw Windows process log. This is an error because the attacker encoded the entire command into random Base64 characters. A real SIEM would never find that plain text word until it's decoded.

SOLUTION: To dodge this problem we have to tune the sigma rule make it look for the stealth flags instead.
detection:
  selection:
    EventID: 1
    Image|endswith: '\powershell.exe'
    CommandLine|contains|all:
      - '-w hidden'
      - '-enc'
  condition: selection

And tune the KQL rule to make it look for the text after the windows decodes it.
DeviceEvents
| where ActionType == "PowerShellCommand" or AdditionalFields.EventId == 4104
| where ScriptBlockText has_any ("DownloadString", "Invoke-Expression", "IEX")
